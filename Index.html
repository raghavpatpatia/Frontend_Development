About Us

Welcome to Dream travels Pvt. Ltd., Delhi based travel Franchise firm which has built strong reputation in competitive marketplace through quality package and matchless services. Dream Travels has maintained strong relationship with all stakeholders: employees, franchisees, and customers.

Our legacy

Every day, we try to make people happy with our services; we consistently provide the best services and take immense pride in presenting the package and information in organized format. It was all started when Mr. Prashant Kulkarni (MBA & Ex Infosys employee) and Mrs. Vinaya Kulakrni (M.Tech & Ex Infosys employee) saw local vendors did not care about cleanliness and freshness.of the people who aur going through their company . They decided to bring standard travel format, with low investment Dream travels was started on streets of Delhi . The First franchise was opened in Gujarat and since then it is consistently transforming travels sector

Providing different type of packages in organized format

We have developed 21+ option of taking packages in different aroma, and texture. We put efforts to produce exceptional option every single minute. The type of service and options which we are proving are special packages available only at Dream travels

An inspiring future

We not only focus on money but also took responsibility to build a better future by offering valuable business opportunities. We are passionate to bring innovations with effective concepts that would revolutionize the whole travel sector around the globe. We are also committed to improve the life of communities around us.
